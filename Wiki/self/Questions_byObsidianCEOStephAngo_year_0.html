Questions by Obsidian CEO Steph Ango Year

Year 每年

  1. What did you do this year that you’d never done before?
    你今年做了哪些之前从未做过的事？

  2. Did you keep your new year’s resolutions?
    你有没有遵守年初时和自己许下的约定？

  3. Did anyone close to you give birth?
    你身边有孩子出生吗？

  4. Did anyone close to you die?
    你身边有人去世吗？

  5. What cities/states/countries did you visit?
    你去了哪些城市/州/国家？

  6. What would you like to have next year that you lacked this year?
    明年你想要获得哪些你今年没有的东西？

  7. What date(s) from this year will remain etched upon your memory, and why?
    今年的哪个或哪些日子会铭刻在你的记忆中，为什么？

  8. What was your biggest achievement of the year?
    你今年最大的成就是什么？

  9. What was your biggest failure?
    你今年最大的失败是什么？

  10. What other hardships did you face?
    你今年还遇到过哪些困难？

  11. Did you suffer illness or injury?
    你今年是否生过病或受过伤？

  12. What was the best thing you bought?
    你今年买过的最好的东西是什么？

  13. Whose behavior merited celebration?
    谁的行为值得表扬？

  14. Whose behavior made you appalled?
    谁的行为令你感到震惊？

  15. Where did most of your money go?
    你大部分的钱都花到哪里去了？

  16. What did you get really, really, really excited about?
    有什么事让你感到超级、超级、超级兴奋？

  17. What song will always remind you of this year?
    哪首歌会永远让你想起这一年？

  18. Compared to this time last year, are you: happier or sadder? Thinner or fatter? Richer or poorer?
    与去年的这个时候相比，你是：感到更快乐还是更悲伤了？变得更瘦还是更胖了？变得更富还是更穷了？

  19. What do you wish you’d done more of?
    你希望自己能做得更多的是什么？

  20. What do you wish you’d done less of?
    你希望自己能做得更少的是什么？

  21. How are you spending the holidays?
    你是如何度过节假日的？

  22. Did you fall in love this year?
    你今年坠入爱河了吗？

  23. Do you hate anyone now that you didn’t hate this time last year?
    你是否有讨厌某个你去年此时不觉得讨厌的人呢？

  24. What was your favorite show?
    你最喜欢的电视节目是什么？

  25. What was the best book you read?
    你读过最好的一本书是什么？

  26. What was your greatest musical discovery of the year?
    你今年发现的最好听的一首歌是什么？

  27. What was your favorite film?
    你今年看过最喜欢的一部电影是什么？

  28. What was your favorite meal?
    你今年吃过最好吃的一顿饭是什么？

  29. What did you want and get?
    有什么是你想要且得到了的？

  30. What did you want and not get?
    有什么是你想要却没有得到的？

  31. What did you do on your birthday?
    你生日那天做了什么？

  32. What one thing would have made your year immeasurably more satisfying?
    有什么还未发生的事，如果发生了，会让你的这一年变得无比满足?

  33. How would you describe your personal fashion this year?
    你会如何描述你今年的个人时尚风格？

  34. What kept you sane?
    是什么让你保持理智？

  35. Which celebrity/public figure did you admire the most?
    你最欣赏哪个名人/公众人物？

  36. What political issue stirred you the most?
    哪个政治问题最令你有感而发？

  37. Who did you miss?
    你想念哪些人？

  38. Who was the best new person you met?
    在你新认识的人之中，谁是最好的？

  39. What valuable life lesson did you learn this year?
    今年你学到了什么宝贵的人生经验？

  40. What is a quote that sums up your year?
    能够总结你这一年的一句话是什么？
